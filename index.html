Privacy Policy — PicNova Reporting Tool

Last updated: 2026-07-14

This privacy policy describes how Goujie Tech ("we", "us") handles data accessed through our internal use of the TikTok Marketing API.

What data we access

Through the TikTok Marketing API, our internal reporting tool ("PicNova Reporting Tool") accesses advertising performance data from our own TikTok Ads advertiser account(s), including but not limited to: campaign, ad group, and ad-level metrics (impressions, clicks, spend, conversions), and creative-level performance data (video completion rate, engagement metrics).

How we use this data

This data is used exclusively for internal analysis and optimization of our own advertising campaigns. It is not used for any purpose beyond our own marketing operations.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose this data to any third party. Access is limited to authorized personnel within our own organization.

Data storage

Data pulled via the API is stored on our own internal infrastructure and is not hosted on any third-party or public-facing service.

Third-party accounts

We do not manage or access advertiser accounts on behalf of any third party. This tool operates solely on our own TikTok Ads advertiser account(s).

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: admin@goujietech.xyz
